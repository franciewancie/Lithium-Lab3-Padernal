Genshin Impact

A traveller who travels the different world with their twin, but gets separated during a trip to the land of teyvat. Now, they have to help the different Archon's problems in exchange for information about their twin. Only one archon helped though. They also meet different characters to help them fight.

5 reasons why this game is fun for me :b

  • The game's writing/storyline is great.
  • The game has alot of content to explore.
  • The game's wishing system is top tier.
  • The game's character designs are pretty.
  • The game's combat style is fun.
